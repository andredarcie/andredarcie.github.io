- RESOLVED (verified mathematically, do not flip signs again): in this engine yaw INCREASES to the LEFT. Proof: `forward=(sin yaw,0,cos yaw)` with camera behind player means screen-right is -X at yaw=0 and d(forward)/d(yaw)=+X=screen-left; keyboard A (left) sets `moveX=+1`; the working mouse path does `yaw -= movementX*sens`. Therefore "lookX>0 = turn right" requires `cameraRig.yaw -= input.lookX*dt` in `updateCamera`. Commit 2670e7f ("fix") broke it by changing `-=` to `+=`; now restored to `-=` with stick mapping `input.lookX = v.x*YAW_SPEED` unchanged.
